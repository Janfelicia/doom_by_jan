About Us

Kleang Of Vinatge is a vintage clothing store in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, called Kleang of Vintange. We have a physical location and an Instagram presence (@kleangofvintage). We appear to focus on high-quality vintage clothing choices. Kleang of Vintange is the place where we study Dress code and Fashion Design, which inspires us to become KICKS.THEWORLDS and DOOM.
